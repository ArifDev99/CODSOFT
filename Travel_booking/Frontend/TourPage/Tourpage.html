Things you need to know

Before you go

What's included

  • Everyday commute by a shared Passenger Van and ferries; all inner line permits for the trip, driver night charges, toll tax, and parking charges.
  • In per day tour we give time for breakfast , lunches time in registered hotel. Meals cost you have to pay . Note: Since evenings are reserved for DIY exploration, we have not included dinners for all days

What's not included

  • Any kind of food items or beverages that are not included in the package, like alcoholic drinks, mineral water, refreshments/lunches on the highway.
  • International flights from India to Phuket and back Note: Airport transfers (Pickup and Drop facility) are not included in the trip package.
  • Any cost arising due to natural calamities.

What to carry

  • Universal travel adapters, Small locks to secure your belongings, Zip locks or plastic bags, Sunscreen or moisturizer, personal meds, and toiletries.
  • Passport, Visa (Original and copy), Flight ticket (printout or e-ticket), and Cash (Converted into Thai Baht). Optional: Driving License & International Driving License (For Tuk Tuks or scooters)
